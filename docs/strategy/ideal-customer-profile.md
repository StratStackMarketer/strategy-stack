## Primary Persona: Amy Loaz

### Overview

She wants to quit her 9-5 but she doesn't know how to proceed, what to do to make a living online. She struggles to find the best idea & she has the shiny object syndrome.

---

## Dream State Analysis

*Instructions: Imagine your ideal customer's dream situation, one in which all their problems have been solved.*

### What emotions would she feel?

*(Define the emotional transformation your customer seeks)*

- Freedom from the 9-5 grind
- Confidence in their business direction
- Financial independence
- Work-life balance
- Pride in building something of their own

---

### What are the biggest obstacles preventing her from changing her situation?

**Mental Blocks:**

- Analysis paralysis — can't decide on "the best" idea
- Shiny object syndrome — constantly distracted by new opportunities
- Fear of failure / leaving stable income
- Lack of clarity on next steps
- Overwhelm from too much information

---

### What would be the ideal solution for which she would be willing to pay any price?

*(Define the transformational outcome)*

- A clear, proven roadmap to online income
- Personalized guidance to cut through the noise
- Accountability and support system
- Systematic approach that eliminates guesswork
- Quick wins that build momentum and confidence

---

## How Strategy Stack Serves This Customer

Strategy Stack helps customers like Amy by:

1. **Cutting through the noise** — Focused strategy, not endless tactics
2. **Providing clear direction** — Step-by-step marketing systems
3. **Eliminating guesswork** — AI-powered insights and proven frameworks
4. **Building confidence** — Measurable progress and quick wins
5. **Ongoing support** — Partnership approach, not just services
